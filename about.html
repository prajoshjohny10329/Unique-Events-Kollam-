About Unique Events

Our Story

From a Passion for Cooking to a Legacy of Excellence

Unique Events is one of Kerala’s top on-site catering services, renowned for our exceptional amenities and hospitality. With 35 years of expertise, we take pride in delivering customized menus that cater to every taste and occasion.

Our journey began in 1990, when our parents founded a small catering business driven by a passion for great food and impeccable service. Over the years, we have grown into a certified catering company, offering a diverse range of cuisines tailored to client preferences.

At Unique Events , freshness is our priority. From our authentic Indian spices for Biryani to our signature South Indian delicacies, every dish is crafted to perfection. We believe that the success of an event lies in perfectly timed and flavorful dining experiences.

Having catered for over 5,000 weddings, engagements, and family gatherings across Kerala, we continue to create unforgettable culinary moments for our clients.
